## Location Features

* Browser geolocation support
* "Use My Location" button
* Automatic nearby job recommendations
* Radius search (5, 10, 25, 50 miles)
* Distance-based filtering
* Graceful fallback when location permission is denied

# Week 8 - AI Assistant Expansion

## Interview Learning Center

Separate from the main AI chatbot.

### Learning Materials

* Interview tips library
* STAR method guide
* Beginner interview guidance
* Virtual interview tips
* First job interview guidance
* College student interview guidance
* Career transition guidance

### Sample Interviews

For every interview question include:

* Question prompt
* Poor response example
* Explanation of why the response is weak
* Strong response example

* Common mistakes section

### Practice Features

* Lightweight practice mode
* Full mock interviews
* Follow-up questions

### Interview Evaluations

* Final interview evaluation report
* Authenticity scoring
